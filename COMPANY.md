# Melaa — Company Context

## What We Do
Melaa (melaa.ca) is Canada's #1 South Asian wedding vendor directory. We connect South Asian couples in Ontario with local wedding vendors across 33 categories and 55+ cities.

## Mission
Make it effortless for South Asian families in Canada to find, compare, and book trusted wedding vendors in their community.

## Product
- **Live website**: melaa.ca
- **Tech stack**: Next.js 15, TypeScript, Supabase (PostgreSQL), Vercel, Tailwind CSS
- **Database**: 2,700+ vendor listings, 33 categories, 55+ Ontario cities
- **Email**: Resend API (hello@melaa.ca)
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics G-7DQS2RPKVN

## Revenue Model
1. **Vendor Premium Listings** — Free / $29 / $79 / $149 per month tiers
2. **Digital Products** — Google Sheets (Wedding Planner $15–25, Budget Tracker $9–15) sold on Etsy/Gumroad
3. **Google Ads** — Drive couple traffic, convert to vendor sign-ups
