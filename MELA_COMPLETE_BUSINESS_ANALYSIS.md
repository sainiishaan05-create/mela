## PRICING AUDIT

**The pricing mistake costing the most revenue:**
Charging the same price regardless of category. A wedding photographer booking one client through Mela earns $3,500. A makeup artist earns $800. The photographer should pay more — and will, if framed correctly. Introduce category-based pricing in Month 4: photographers and decorators at $149/$249/$349, all other categories at $99/$199/$299.

**The psychological pricing adjustment:**
Change $199/month to $197/month. Change $299/month to $297/month. The left-digit effect is real — $197 feels meaningfully cheaper than $199 even though the difference is $2. More importantly, $297 looks less like a round arbitrary number and more like a calculated price.

**The price point leaving money on the table:**
There is no annual option. A vendor paying $199/month would pay $1,788/year. Offer annual at $1,490 (save $298 = 2 free months). You receive cash upfront, they save money, churn drops to near zero for 12 months. Introduce this on Day 60.
